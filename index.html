World Happiness Report


The World Happiness Report 2017 ranks 155 countries by their happiness levels. The reports review the state of happiness in the world today and show how the new science of happiness explains personal and national variations in happiness. The report also describes the extent to which factors like GDP per Capita, Family, Life Expectancy, contribute in evaluating the happiness in each country.
